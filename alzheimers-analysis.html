Dataset Description

Features in the Dataset:

  • Demographic Factors

    • Diabetes: Presence of diabetes (Yes/No)
    • Hypertension: Presence of high blood pressure (Yes/No)
    • Cholesterol Level: Cholesterol status
  • Cognitive & Psychological Factors
    • Family History of Alzheimerâ€™s: Whether a family member had Alzheimerâ€™s
    • Cognitive Test Score: A numerical cognitive ability assessment
    • Depression Level: Scale of depressive symptoms
    • Sleep Quality: Rating of sleep patterns
  • Environmental & Social Factors
    • Dietary Habits: Nutritional quality of diet
    • Air Pollution Exposure: Level of exposure to pollutants
    • Employment Status: Employed, Unemployed, Retired
    • Marital Status: Single, Married, Divorced, Widowed
    • Genetic Risk Factor (APOE-Îµ4 allele): Presence of high-risk allele
    • Social Engagement Level: Interaction frequency with social networks
    • Income Level: Income category
    • Stress Levels: Self-reported stress level
    • Urban vs Rural Living: Living in urban vs rural areas

    • Alzheimers Diagnosis: Whether the individual is diagnosed with Alzheimerâ€™s

Each observation represents an individual and their corresponding health and lifestyle data.
